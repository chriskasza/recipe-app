Chipotle Black Bean Tacos

4.8 (214) 25 min Serves 4

A weeknight hero that comes together in under half an hour. Smoky chipotle black beans pile into warm tortillas with charred corn, quick-pickled red onion, and a cooling lime crema — the kind of dinner that tastes like you fussed when you really didn't.

Vegan High-protein 30 minutes or less One pan

Preparation time

  • Total: approximately 25 minutes
  • Preparation: 10 minutes
  • Cooking: 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 tins (400g each) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 2 tsp chipotle paste, plus more to taste
  • 1 small red onion, half finely sliced for pickling
  • 1 corn cob, kernels sliced off (or 150g frozen)
  • 8 small corn or flour tortillas, warmed
  • 1 lime, plus extra wedges to serve
  • 4 tbsp soy or coconut yoghurt, for the crema
  • 1 tbsp olive oil, plus a pinch each of cumin and salt
  • Fresh coriander and sliced avocado, to serve

Instructions

  1. Quick-pickle the onion. Toss the sliced red onion with the juice of half a lime and a pinch of salt. Set aside to soften while you cook everything else.
  2. Char the corn. Heat the oil in a large pan over high heat. Add the corn kernels and cook, undisturbed, for 3–4 minutes until blistered and golden, then tip onto a plate.
  3. Build the beans. Lower the heat to medium. Add the black beans, chipotle paste, cumin and a splash of water. Simmer for 6–8 minutes, mashing lightly, until thick and glossy.
  4. Make the crema. Stir the yoghurt with the remaining lime juice and a pinch of salt until pourable.
  5. Assemble. Spoon the beans into warm tortillas. Top with charred corn, pickled onion, avocado and coriander, then finish with crema and a squeeze of lime.
